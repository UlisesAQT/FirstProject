Serving you since 2007!

The Tamayo family’s story begins in Mexico City (CDMX), where their humble beginnings started in the local markets selling clothes. With dreams of a better future, the family began migrating to the United States around 2002 in search of new opportunities.

By 2007, the Tamayo family opened their first restaurant — Tacos El Rancho — marking the start of a new chapter. The restaurant quickly became a local favorite, and between 2007 and 2017, the family expanded, opening three additional locations across Orlando. Tacos El Rancho soon became a staple in the community, known for its authentic Mexican flavors and family atmosphere.

In 2013, the family launched Tamale Co., their first food truck brand, dedicated to sharing traditional tamales and Mexican street food throughout Florida. From 2013 to 2025, Tamale Co. has grown steadily, expanding its fleet of food trucks and opening two restaurant locations — the first in 2016 and the second in 2025 — continuing its success and passion for authentic tamales.

In 2022, the family introduced their third concept, Ta’Chingon, a homestyle sit-down Mexican restaurant located in Winter Park, Florida. Despite being a young brand, Ta’Chingon has quickly made its mark as one of Winter Park’s standout dining destinations.

From selling clothes in the markets of Mexico City to owning several of Orlando’s most beloved Mexican restaurants, the Tamayo family’s journey is one of perseverance, passion, and pride. Today, their restaurants — Tacos El Rancho, Tamale Co., and Ta’Chingon — are all recognized among Orlando’s Top 100 Places to Eat, leaving a lasting foodprint on the city they now call home.

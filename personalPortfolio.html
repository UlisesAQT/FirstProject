Serving you since 2007!

The Tamayo family has made a lasting mark on the City Beautiful. With over seven restaurants across Orlando, their culinary journey began with the one that started it all: Tacos El Rancho, founded in 2007, now thriving with four locations. Next came Tamale Co. in 2013, starting as a humble food truck and growing into two full-service restaurants in Orlando. Most recently, Ta’Chingon joined the family in 2023, bringing bold flavors to Winter Park with its first location. From classic favorites to innovative new concepts, the Tamayo family continues to serve up authentic, delicious Mexican cuisine throughout Orlando.

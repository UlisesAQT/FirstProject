About Tacos el Rancho

Tacos El Rancho is the original restaurant that began the Tamayo family’s journey. Established in 2007, Tacos El Rancho has proudly served the Orlando community for over 18 years, growing from one humble location into four staple restaurants across the city.

Known for its golden chimichanga, authentic tacos, and true Mexican flavor, Tacos El Rancho has become a beloved part of Orlando’s dining scene. With nearly two decades of history, it continues to represent the family’s dedication to quality, authenticity, and sharing the flavors of Mexico with every guest who walks through its doors.

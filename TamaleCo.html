About TamaleCo

Founded in 2013, Tamale Co. began as a humble food truck with a passion for sharing authentic Mexican flavors. What started on four wheels has since grown into two full-service restaurants in the heart of Orlando.

In 2018, Tamale Co. opened its first brick-and-mortar location — a fast-casual Mexican restaurant in the Hourglass District on Curry Ford and Bumby. Building on that success, a second location opened in 2025: a full-service, sit-down Mexican restaurant with a complete bar, located in the vibrant College Park district on Edgewater Drive.

You may have already experienced Tamale Co.’s signature tamales without even realizing it — they’re proudly served at Epcot’s Mexico Pavilion in Walt Disney World. Beyond Disney, Tamale Co. has catered to some of the nation’s most innovative organizations, including SpaceX, NASA, Boeing, and AdventHealth.
From its modest beginnings as a food truck to becoming a beloved staple in the Orlando community, Tamale Co. continues to serve up the same authentic flavors, family recipes, and dedication that started it all.
